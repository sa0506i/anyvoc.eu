2. Subject matter

Anyvoc is a vocabulary-learning tool that runs locally on your device. From submitted content (texts, images, web links), it extracts and translates vocabulary using AI-assisted language analysis and trains it with a spaced-repetition system (Leitner).

The app is offered in two tiers:

  • Free (free of charge, indefinitely) — fully usable with certain volume limits and restricted to one learning language at a time.
